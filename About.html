    • Welcome to Spellbuild !

    Spellbuild is a new multiplayer third person shooter online game. Spellbuild has a unique style of battle royale system. Spellbuild uses gauntlet as a weapon and dealing damage with magic which is very unique and challenging.
    60 Players will be dropping down from the sky and roam around the canyon to find items and weapons that will guide them to other players and fight them until they reach victory.
    Each team contains of 3 mages that will work together to the end.









    King's Canyon, California. Year 2186.

    The events of Spellbuild take place in Kings Canyon National Park located at California, United States. Kings Canyon is also home to Redwood Canyon, the largest remaining grove of sequoia trees in the world. Everything changed as if nothing happened. Magic to the world and filled the world with lots of magical stuff. There was a magic war that happened in 2148. The impact is still going until now. People are becoming battlemages to fight each other until the last man is standing.








    Be the Mage you have always wanted.

    Mystic

    A fire persona that will burn anything with her fists that stands in her way. Known for the Fiery Ocean ability.

    Sorcerer

    Champion
